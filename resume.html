WORK EXPERIENCE

After receiving my B.A., I worked for Coopers & Lybrand proofing corporate and personal tax returns. Then I moved to International Sports Propteries (1980) Inc. (ISPI) where I was promoted over time from receptionist to editor of several ISPI publications. I assigned, proofed, and wrote articles as well as scheduled upcoming issues of various publications, including: CFL Illustrated; The (Toronto Maple) Leafs Magazine; and The (Winnipeg) Jets Magazine.

I also edited publications for The Royal Agricultural Winter Fair; Ontario Place and The Forum; Canada's Wonderland and Kingswood Music Theatre.

I left ISPI to attend law school. After being called to the Bar, I practiced for a brief time, primarily in the area of enternatinment law as a sole proprietor. I no longer practise law and do not provide legal advice, legal research or legal services. My focus has been on writing for publications.

CPA CANADA

In 1994, I became the managing editor of The Accountant's Manual (now the Canadian Business Compliance Manual (CBCM), published by the CICA (Canadian Institute of Chartered Acccounts - now CPA Canada). The Manual aims to provide an overview in straightforward language of basic legislative and other information on a national basis that is relevant to businesses in 29 areas of law and related topics.

In August 2000 I became co-author of CBCM and other CICA publications including: The Family Trust Guide, Model Agreements, Resolutions and Contracts, The Personal Tax Planner Guide, The Professional Services Checklist Collection, Tax Risk Management Guide (formerly Tax Practice Manual), Tax Planning Using Family Trusts, and The Registered Charities Guide.

CBCM and The Registered Charities Guide are no longer published by CPA Canada. My co-author and I retain the rights to these publications.

I have also contributed to various versions of A Guide to Financial Decisions: Planning for the End of Life, which was most recently published by CPA Canada in 2016.

OTHER PUBLISHERS

I am also a pubilshed poet of The Eel Ladder (Watershed Books, 1999) and I Think It's Time to Give Up on Henry (Lyrical Myrical Press, 2006).
